Services Offered

Saluda Valley Guides offers a variety of services, including full day and half day floats as well as private casting lessons.

Service Rate
8 hour Full Day Float $500.00
4 hour Half Day Float $350.00
2 hour Private Casting Lesson $80.00
Float Trips- Our most popular option, allows up to two guests to fish in the least accessible sections of the Saluda River, Broad River and Congaree River
  • Float trips are limited to two guests but the price is the same for one or two guests
  • Lunch is not included on full day trips. However, a cooler with ice and bottled water will be on board.
  • 50% deposit is required upon booking.
    • Deposit is forfeited if canceled 48 hrs or less prior to trip date.
Lessons - You can make them private or get some friends to join you
  • $40.00 each additional guest
Rates do not include gratuity
